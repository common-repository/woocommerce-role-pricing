== Description ==

Unlock the power of dynamic pricing in your WooCommerce store! Tailor your prices to fit the unique needs of different user roles, from registered users and subscribers to editors and more. With our plugin, you can create a personalized shopping experience that rewards your customers, encourages loyalty, and boosts your sales.

=== New feature ===
Now, even visitors who aren't logged in can benefit from discounts. Instantly apply store-wide discounts with just a few clicks.

= Features: =
  * Flexibility in discount types: choose between percentage-based or fixed amount discounts.
  * Apply discounts to the regular or sale prices.
  * Customizable discounts based on the user's role, ensuring a tailored shopping experience.
  * Options to apply either discounts or specific amounts, offering greater pricing control.
  * Enhance user experience by hiding prices and the "Add to Cart" button from unregistered users, encouraging sign-ups.

= Pro version: =
  * Discounts by products (now variables available)
  * Discounts by categories
  * If user has various roles, you can select the criterial (higher discount, lower discount, ...)

== Installation ==

You can either install it automatically from the WordPress admin, or do it manually:

1. Unzip the archive and put the `woocommerce-role-pricing` folder into your plugins folder (/wp-content/plugins/).
2. Activate the plugin from the Plugins menu.
3. In Woocommerce->Role Pricing you can setup the plugin.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Only rate method is available to discounts ? =
No, you can use rate or a fixed amount as discount.
= I want to apply different discounts by product. =
You need Pro version to get this feature.
= I select apply to sale price but my product isn't on sale. =
Then discount is applied to the regular price.
